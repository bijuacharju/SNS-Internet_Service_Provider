About Us

Sunsari Network and Service Pvt. Ltd. is a private broadband internet service provider with 9 years of experience.

Sunsari Network Service (SNS) is a prominent Internet and Network Service Provider in Nepal with over 500 direct and indirect employees rendering their service throughout Nepal. Founded in 2002 A.D with the aim of providing Internet Service with then a reputed name of Sunsari Network Service Pvt. Ltd. has started to provide internet service as Sunsari Network Service in 2007 A.D under present Director ---------------. In 2012 A.D SNS started to provide full internet service over a dedicated leased-line in Nepal. Today SNS holds a good reputation with 50Gbps of Internet bandwidth with Network spanning at more than 50 locations in Nepal and is gradually expanding its network with an aim to cover all the districts of Nepal.
